Did You Know?

Termites cause billions in property damage annually and can silently destroy wooden structures for years before being detected — by the time signs are visible, significant damage may already be done.

That's why effective termite control requires a thorough structural assessment, targeted colony elimination treatments, and preventive barriers to protect your property from future termite activity.

ZAPS SOLUTIONS Effective Approach for Termite Control

Structural Assessment

Conduct a thorough assessment of foundations, walls, and wooden structures to detect active termite colonies and entry points.

Colony Elimination

Apply targeted treatments including baiting systems and liquid termiticides to eliminate termite colonies at the source.

Soil Treatment

Treat soil around the structure to create a protective barrier that prevents termites from reaching and damaging your property.

Long-Term Protection

Regular monitoring and follow-up treatments ensure your property stays protected from future termite infestations.
